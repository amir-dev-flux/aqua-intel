## Problem

Saudi Arabia faces extreme water scarcity and climate pressure on agriculture. Efficient irrigation and data-driven farming are critical to food security and Vision 2030 sustainability goals.

## Project Goal

Build an AI-powered platform that:

- Predicts irrigation water needs
- Analyzes climate patterns
- Uses ML/DL to optimize water usage
- Supports sustainable agriculture in arid regions
